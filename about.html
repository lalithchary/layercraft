About LayerCraft

A 3D printing workshop built for makers, engineers, and creators across India.

Our Story

We started LayerCraft because getting quality 3D prints in India shouldn't be expensive, slow, or complicated.

As makers ourselves, we know the frustration of needing a quick prototype and facing week-long wait times or paying premium prices for simple PLA parts. We built the service we wished existed — fast turnaround, transparent pricing, and genuine care for every print.

Whether you need a single prototype for your college project or a batch of 100 parts for your startup, we handle it with the same precision and attention to detail.

Our Mission

Make professional 3D printing accessible to every maker, student, engineer, and creator in India — without the premium price tag or complexity.
